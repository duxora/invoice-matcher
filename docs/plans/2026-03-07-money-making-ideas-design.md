## Idea A: AI Automation Agency for SMBs

**Revenue:** $2K-5K setup + $500-1.5K/month retainer per client
**Effort:** Medium (2-3 clients = $3K-6K/month)

### Concept
Offer done-for-you AI workflow automation to small businesses. Use your Claude Max plan + automation-hub to build, deploy, and maintain workflows.

### Services
1. **Workflow audit** — Analyze client's manual processes, identify automation opportunities
2. **Build automations** — Use Claude to generate scripts, integrations, and workflows
3. **Deploy & maintain** — Self-hosted on client infra or your own servers
4. **Monthly optimization** — Review performance, add new workflows

### Why Claude Max helps
- Extended Thinking for complex business logic analysis
- Long context for reading entire codebases/docs at once
- Memory for maintaining client context across sessions
- Build faster → higher margins

### How to start
1. Create a portfolio with 3 example automations (use your automation-hub)
2. Post on Upwork/Fiverr: "AI Workflow Automation Specialist"
3. Target: agencies, e-commerce, real estate, legal firms
4. Deliverable: working automation + documentation + training video
